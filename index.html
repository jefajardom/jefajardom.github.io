Hi!

My name is Jaime Fajardo. I am a mechatronic engineer with a Master's in Engineering from the University of Toronto, where I specialized in Robotics and ELITE, Before diving into graduate studies, I spent over five years in the automotive industry as an Automation and Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) Engineer. I designed and built testing platforms from the ground up—both hardware and software—to validate vehicle modules and ensure they performed flawlessly. I’ve always enjoyed the challenge of making complex systems work seamlessly together. My passion lies at the intersection of robotics, automation, and AI—especially when they’re used to build real-world solutions that improve lives and make a positive impact on society.
